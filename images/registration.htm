Registration

The registration form is available as a .pdf file: registration_2013

To use the .pdf file: install the free Acrobat Reader from Adobe in order to see the form.

If you are not able to open the pdf file, we have provided a .jpg image file. To use this file to register:
1. Save this file to your computer. (Windows: right click on the link below, select <save target as>, browse to a folder, save)
2. Open your word processing program
3. Insert, place, or import this picture on the page.
4. Grab the image (by the corners only) to resize and fill the page.

Finally, print, fill out, and make a copy of the completed form for your records before mailing to the address on the form. Remember to include either the deposit or full amount for your registration. Thank you.

We look forward to seeing you at The Barstow Institute.
